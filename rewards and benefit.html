Rewards & Benefits

Rewards for Consumers

Green Contribution Bonus:

Consumers receive bonus payments or reward points for contributing high-quality or bulk recyclable items like smartphones, laptops or recycling weight milestones (e.g., after 5 pickups or 25kg).

Surprise Recycle Boxes:

Send users surprise goodies like seed paper bookmarks, reusable straws, eco-bags, or even digital coupons for random recycling milestones..

Loyalty Points System:

Users collect points for each recycling transaction, redeemable for vouchers or rewards..

Benefits for Consumers

Free Pickup for Loyal Recyclers:

Enjoy complimentary waste pickups as a thank-you for your consistency.The more you recycle, the more perks you unlock like zero pickup charges..

Sustainability Badges:

Earn digital badges and eco-certificates for your green contributions. Celebrate milestones like "Tree Saver" or "Plastic-Free Hero.".
